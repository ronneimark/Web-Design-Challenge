Latitude vs Temperature

As expected, the closer the city was to the Equator, the higher the temperature. At first blush, it appears that the northern hemisphere is colder, but on second glance it becomes apparent that there are no readings in the southern hemisphere between 60° and 90° and very few between 40° and 60°, as there are either very few cities identified by citypy or very few weather stations reporting to OpenWeather.
